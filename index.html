XYZW Club is a lunchtime get-together for people to learn and play with all things 3d-rendered.

Some of the things we're thinking of covering:

  • Writing a simple software 3d engine
  • Simple maths behind 3d transforms and projection
  • Getting started with WebGL
  • Raytracing and raymarching
  • Deferred rendering pipelines
  • Physically-based rendering
  • And more...

This is absolutely not an expert group. Come along to learn, to teach, to play with a different set of programs and problems than you get in your day job, or just to eat your lunch.
